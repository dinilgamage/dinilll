Zesta

Zesta is your start to an extraordinary Ceylon Tea experience. Right from the moment of the picking of two leaves and bud, to the orthodox method of manufacturing that has been perfected over centuries, Zesta is not only fresh, it is also filled with the passion and expertise that our teamakers put into bringing you the perfect cup of tea. Sourced from tea estates in Sri Lanka our teas are rich in character which is why both local and consumers around the world have enjoyed Zesta tea for over a century.
